# Summer Steak
## **Ingredients**
- 1lb Steak of your choice
- 2 tbsp serrano chili flakes. Can substitute for other chili flakes just watch out for how spicy they are.
- 2 tangerines juiced
- 4 cloves garlic roughly chopped
- 1.5 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp canola or other neutral, high smoke point oil
- 2 tbsp butter
- Kosher salt
- Finishing salt (fleur de sel or similar)
## **Meat Prep**
1. Apply a liberal amount of kosher salt to your steak as far in advance as possible before cooking
2. Remove steak from fridge 30-60 minutes
## **Marinade (Added To Steak Day of Cooking)**
1. Juice 2 tangerines into a bowl large enough to marinade steak
2. Add 2 tbsp chili flakes, 1.5 tbsp olive oil to marinade
3. Stir until homogenous
4. Add steak to bowl and leave to marinade for no longer than 1 day
## **Cooking Instructions**
1. Heat a cast iron, stainless or carbon steel pan (prefered) on medium heat until oil smokes in the pan
2. Add 1tsp canola oil (or other high smoke point oil), then immediately add steak. NOTE: If your steak has a fat cap, place this side on the pan first
3. Sear steak on high heat on all sides
4. Once all sides are seared, add 2 tbsp butter and garlic
5. Baste in melted garlic butter until desired internal temp
